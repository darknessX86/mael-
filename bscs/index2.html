Events

Clean-up drive 🌱

On September 20, 2025, the University of Manila actively participated in the International Coastal Clean-Up Drive held at By the Bay. A total of 71 participants—composed of faculty members, non-teaching staff, and students from the Colleges of Computer Science, Japanology, and Foreign Service—volunteered their time and effort for this meaningful environmental initiative.

Outreach program 💙

November 26, 2023 CWTS/NSTP 1&2 1ST SEMESTER S.Y. 2023-2024 CWTS/NSTP of The University of Manila conducted an outreach program for the indigenous people of Sitio Katwiran in Barangay San Rafael, Rodriguez Rizal. Around 95 children received bath towel, comb, toothbrush, toothpaste, body soap, shampoo, baby powder, polo t-shirt, shirts, joggers and slippers.

Film festival 🎬

The University of Manila faculty & students attended the Japanese Film Festival in February 4, 2024. Thank you, Japan Foundation Manila, for the invitation.

About Us

Motto

To acquire knowledge and skills for the benefit of mankind

Vision

To impart to the future generations the university of manila trilogy of ideals: love of country, love of science and love of virtue, in making the lives of others better, brighter, lighter and more liveable by making quality education accessible to all.

Mission

ACE - to Accomplish something with complete success.

A - Anchor in the hearts of the youth the love of country, science, and virtue.

C - Create an atmosphere of academic excellence and competence.

E - Empower the youth with modern scientific technological skills in braving the challenges of the global society.

History

Established as instituto de manila by a group of filipino educators, 5 october 1913. converted into a university, 3 november 1921. apolinario g. de los santos, first president. opened first school of foreign service, 3 may 1946. building was occupied by the japanese forces during their occupation of manila. used temporarily as city hall and senate session hall in 1945. mariand v. de los santos, president since 1927.
